About me
Who I am

A developer learning by doing.

I'm César, known online as ZorCeDev. I studied cross-platform application development (DAM) for two years, which gave me a solid foundation in software architecture, clean code and design patterns. Now I'm one year deep into Unity, and I'm fully hooked.

I'm just starting out — my first game is a school project, my first asset just hit the Unity Asset Store. But everything I ship is something I've thought hard about, built carefully, and tested myself. I care about doing things properly from day one.

My goal is to keep making games, keep learning, and share useful tools with developers along the way.
